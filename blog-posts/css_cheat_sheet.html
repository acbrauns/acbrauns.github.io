CSS Cheat Sheet

This is a list of all inherited attributes in CSS. While some attributes are inherited by the child element from the parent, some are not! Margin and padding, as you will notice, are not on this list. This is why the selector body{margin: 0} would not change the margin of the paragraphs within it.
